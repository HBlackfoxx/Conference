Manuscript Topics

The automotive and aeronautical industries are undergoing a profound digital transformation, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to enhance efficiency, resilience, and sustainability. This special issue explores both classical and advanced industrial engineering and logistics methods applied in these sectors, focusing on how digitalization is reshaping supply chain management, production systems, and operational strategies.
