Call for Special Sessions - 8th International Conference on Industrial and Logistics Systems (SIL’25)

The 8th International Conference on Industrial and Logistics Systems (SIL’25) invites proposals for special sessions within the technical scope of the conference. Special sessions will complement the regular program by highlighting state-of-the-art research in both academia and industry, focusing on novel, emerging, and challenging topics.

Special session proposals should be submitted by prospective organizers who will be responsible for promoting and managing the review process of their session as Chairs or Co-Chairs.

Proposals should include the following information:

  • Title
  • Name(s) of organizer(s)
  • Email of the main contact person
  • Brief bio(s) of organizer(s)
  • Description
  • Related topics

Please submit the proposal via email to: t.benazzouz@uca.ac.ma

Important Dates:

  • Submission of Special Sessions’ Proposal: April 1, 2025
  • Notification of the Proposal: April 15, 2025
  • Complete Set of Special Session: April 30, 2025
