Awards

The conference will recognize outstanding contributions with the following awards, which will be announced and presented during the closing session:

Best Industrial Paper Award

Awarded to the most impactful paper showcasing industrial applications, innovations, or case studies.

Best Paper Award

Only full paper submissions presented at the conference will be eligible for this award.

Best PhD Project Award

To qualify, the first author must be a registered PhD student at a university. Only full paper submissions presented at the conference will be considered.

Outstanding Organizing Award

Given to special session chairs in recognition of their exceptional efforts in organizing and contributing to the success of the conference.
